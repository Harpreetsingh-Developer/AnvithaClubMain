    Prof. Satyendra Sharma has received Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IEI, Calcutta, India, Master of Engineering with specialization in VLSI Design from UPTU - Lucknow and Ph.D. from NIT Kurukshetra. He has served 17 yrs. in Indian air force for installation, operation & maintenance of various radars and communications equipment’s. His aim in life is ‘Work is Worship’. He has authored 41 research papers in reputed journals. He has held several administrative and academic positions during 42 years of experience. His current research interests include network planning, designing and optimization of cellular networks. He is a member of ISTE and AMIE.
